About Us

India's North East (INE) Exotic Foods Private Limited is dedicated to bringing the authentic flavours of India’s North East to national and global markets through advanced food processing technology that retains nutrients, colour, and flavour without artificial preservatives.

Mission

To elevate indigenous foods of India’s North East using modern, clean-label technology while empowering local farming communities.

Vision

To become a leading global brand for high-quality indigenous food products by blending traditional resources with cutting-edge food innovation.

Problem Statement

Despite an abundance of unique crops, India’s North East faces significant post-harvest losses due to limited modern food processing infrastructure. Traditional methods often reduce nutritional value, shorten shelf life, and restrict market expansion.

Our Solution

INE Exotic Foods uses a novel technology that retains natural nutrients, colour, aroma, and texture while ensuring extended shelf life — all without artificial preservatives.

Products & Innovation

What We Offer

High-quality, clean-label indigenous products.

Key Features

  • Novel technology
  • Retention of natural nutrients, colour, and flavour
  • No preservatives, artificial colours, or additives
  • Enhanced shelf stability with safe, hygienic methods

Advantages

Our Leadership & Technical Team

Yumnam Nandan — Founder & Managing Director

Yumnam Nandan is an entrepreneur and the founder of INE Exotic Foods Private Limited. His entrepreneurial work focuses on developing clean-label indigenous food products, establishing sustainable value chains for India's Northeast agricultural produce, and integrating proprietary processing innovations to bring high-quality products to the market.

He is also a research scholar in the Department of Chemical Engineering at IIT Tirupati, where his academic research focuses on membrane processing for food applications. His research background strengthens the scientific and technical understanding behind food systems.

Prof. KSMS Raghavarao — Senior Scientific Mentor

Former Director of the Central Food Technological Research Institute (CFTRI), Mysore, and Professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering at IIT Tirupati. With more than 30 years of expertise in food engineering, emerging processing technologies, and industrial-scale process development, he provides scientific mentorship, and R&D guidance.

Dr. Praneeth Juvvi — Technical Advisor

Assistant Professor at the College of Food Technology under the Central Agricultural University (CAU). He completed his Ph.D. from CSIR–CFTRI, Mysore, specializing in food science, product stability, and advanced processing methods. He supports the company with technical validation, process refinement, food safety evaluation, and scale-up planning for food product development.
